Óstante Ristorante Italiano is an Italian restaurant located in scenic Rome. Serving classical Italian cuisine, we serve you in a beautiful, cold wine cellar.

We make all of our pasta in house, by hand, before every meal. We make all sorts of pasta, including:

  • Spaghetti
  • Linguine
  • Farfalle
  • Tetrazzini
  • Rigatoni
  • Cannelloni
  • Tortellini

Our impressive selection of wine will keep you so drunk all night that you won't realize that we are extremely overpriced.
